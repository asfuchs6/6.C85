About me

Welcome to my website! I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science and Brain and Cognitive Sciences at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Once I graduate this May, I will have enriched my academic journey by completing courses in Artificial Intelligence, Open Science and Robust Methods, Interactive Data Visualization, and cutting-edge Neurotechnology. My passion for exploring how technology intersects with human cognition has led me to engage in research within Professor Rebecca Saxe's lab, focusing specifically on understanding how infants learn to navigate their environment.

This website showcases my technical journey, ranging from academic class projects to professional industry work. My aim is to share insights, breakthroughs, and innovations that stem from my deep dive into the realms of computer science and cognitive science.

If you're intrigued by my work or see potential collaborations, please don't hesitate to reach out through LinkedIn or email. I'm always open to discussing new ideas, opportunities, and the ways in which we can push the boundaries of technology and understanding together.
